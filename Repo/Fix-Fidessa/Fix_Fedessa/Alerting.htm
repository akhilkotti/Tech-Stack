The alerts we'd like to add for Asia are:

 

1. Odd Lot Alert

We get an algo order from a client and the size is not a multiple of the lot size.

The algo system will only trade the round lot part and leave the odd lot un-traded.

You can get the lot size for each stock from Reuters, or TapMaster / SDM

 

2. Price moving away

The last trade price moves away (up for a buy order or down for a sell) from the order's limit price.

Send an alert once per order when the last trade price moves 3% from the order limit.

 

3. Large price move

The price of the stock has moved by more than 3% since the order was first received

 

4. Large market move

The price of the index has moved by more than 3% since the order was first received

You can get the live index price from Reuters, you just need to have one index defined per market (ASX200 for AU stocks, HSI for HK stocks, etc)

 

5. Large sector move (This is nice to have, but lower priority)

The price of the stocks primary sector moves by more than 3% since the order was first received

You can get the live sector price from Reuters, you just need to have one sector defined per stock
