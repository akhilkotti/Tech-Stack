Trading Interview Questions

Q1) The RTD Software Architecture?

Ans)

The RTD Realtime Trading Desktop is based on an exchange-independent client-server architecture. The RTD Communication Server forms the core of the system and acts as the interface to the RTD and eRTD front-ends as well as to the RTD-API (Application Programing Interface).


The RTD Communication Server allows the integration of multiple exchanges on a single trading platform with a single front-end for trading on all markets. Due to its flexibility and object-oriented software design the RTD architecture has consistently maintained high levels of performance and reliability across the extensive RTS product portfolio further
enhancing RTS’s reputation as the electronic trading platform for the future.


The client-server architecture allows for extremely fast integration of new markets – a considerable advantage in view of the ever changing developments in the world’s
exchanges.

Q6) RTD Connect – Trading Without Membership.?

Ans) RTD Connect links RTS customers with each other securely, reliably and swiftly. This means that clients can access global markets in a cost- effective manner by utilizing the exchange memberships of other customers.

Q7) RTD Net – The Alternative to Individual Network Connections.?

Ans) The RTD Net guarantees a high performance network connection for those users of RTD Connect who do not wish to have their own network infrastructure. In addition to providing an optimal connection, RTD Net also provides the means to control, maintain, set-up and install both the connection and the hardware for the required application.

Q8) RTD-API – One Single Gateway to the Global Markets.?

Ans) The Application Programing Interface (API) allows for flexible and functionally rich communication between the RTD system, worldwide exchanges and in-house applications. In addition, the RTD-API allows the integration of order routing, market making, back office and proprietary trading software as well as the realtime data transfer of orders, trades, positions and market information.

Q9) RTD FIX-API – The worldwide standardized Interface.?

Ans)  On the basis of the FIX-API the simple and fast integration of customer systems is guaranteed. The RTD FIX-API offers central access to all integrated exchanges in RTD with a single API.

Q10) Internet Trading Tool eRTD, Everyone can trade on Electronic Markets?

Ans)The Internet solution eRTD offers retail brokers, day traders and institutional customers a cost-effective and innovative trading application which can be favourably compared with existing professional traders' trading tools with regard to both functionality and reliability.

eRTD allows the user to trade multiple products on multiple exchanges in realtime and from one single screen only.

Q11) mRTD - Define and Implement Individual Market
Models.?

Ans) The matcher solution mRTD allows the efficient design of individual electronic market models.

These characteristics make the mRTD an efficient instrument for centralizing order flows or for selling a bank’s individual products.


The integration of RTD and eRTD as trading applications with mRTD as an internal market creates new, innovative markets. The open RTD architecture allows the mRTD to be used separately from all other RTS products so that it can be used in conjunction with a customer-developed trading solution and other third-party software.


By using the RTD Quote Machine® in combination with mRTD, the internal market can automatically be provided with liquidity. The integrated basic data management in mRTD enables the user to easily administer and maintain the traded instruments.
